Coursework and Majors

At Kenyon I double major in Mathematics and Classics, but my journey began even before that. My senior year of high school, I was dual enrolled at The Ohio State University where I took Calculus III and Intermediate Latin: Cicero.

My first semester I took Linear Algebra, Introduction to Philosophy, Introduction to Ancient Greek, and Introduction to Architecture. The second semester I completed Foundations, Introductory English, and Rhetoric in Antiquity

Sophomore year I continued with Intermediate Ancient Greek, Real Analysis I, Philosophy of Language, and Introduction to Programming. In the second semester of that year I took Real Analysis II, Intermediate Latin, Introductory English, Dynamical Systems, and an independent study in Anthropology

Junior Year (current)

Most Recently, I spent the first semester of my junior year abroad at College Year Athens in Athens, Greece. While there I focused on Classical Studies, taking Advanced Latin and Ancient Greek. I also took Aegean Prehistory in the Bronze Age, Digital Archaeology, and Introduction to Modern Greek. When I wasn't studying or travelling, I volunteered at the Blegen Library of the American School of Classical Studies.
