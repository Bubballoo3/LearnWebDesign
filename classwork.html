Class Work and Majors

My Freshman year I took Linear Algebra, Introduction to Philosophy, Introduction to Ancient Greek, and Introduction to Architecture in the first semester. The second semester I completed Foundations, Introductory English, and Rhetoric in Antiquity

Sophomore year I continued with Intermediate Ancient Greek, Real Analysis I, Philosophy of Language, and Introduction to Programming. In the second semester of that year I took Real Analysis II, Intermediate Latin, Introductory English, Dynamical Systems, and an independent study in Anthropology

The first semester of my junior year was spent abroad at College Year Athens in Athens, Greece. While there I focused on Classical Studies, taking Advanced Latin and Ancient Greek. I also took Aegean Prehistory in the Bronze Age, Digital Archaeology, and Introduction to Modern Greek. When I wasn't studying or travelling, I volunteered at the Blegen Library of the American School of Classical Studies.
